About the Program

The Ready Remote Jobs (RRJ) Ambassador Program is designed for individuals who want to earn commissions by referring others to a platform that genuinely helps people become job-ready and find remote work.

As an ambassador, you don’t need to sell aggressively or manage products. You simply share RRJ with content creators, students, and professionals who need it—and get rewarded.
